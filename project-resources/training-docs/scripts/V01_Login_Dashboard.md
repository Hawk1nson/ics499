# V01 — System Login & Dashboard Overview

**Phase:** 1 — Foundation  
**Audience:** All staff roles  
**Estimated duration:** ~2 minutes  
**Watch first:** None — this is the first video in the series  
**Prerequisites for viewer:** None

---

## Production Setup

- **Login as:** TRIAGE_NURSE (broadest clinical dashboard view)
- **Start screen:** `login.php` — logged out
- **Test data needed:** Any active staff account
- **Note:** Do NOT show a real password. Use a demo credential like `nurse1` / `Demo@1234`.

---

## Script

### [0:00–0:10] Opening shot
> [On screen: login.php — the CareSystem login page]

"Welcome to CareSystem. This video gives you a quick tour of how to log in and what you'll see when you get started."

---

### [0:10–0:30] Logging in
> [Action: Click the username field, type a username. Click the password field, type a password (characters obscured). Click Log In.]

"Enter your username or email address, then your password, and click Log In. Your login is role-based — what you see after logging in depends on the role your administrator assigned to your account."

---

### [0:30–1:00] Dashboard overview
> [On screen: dashboard.php — full dashboard for a TRIAGE_NURSE]

"This is your dashboard. The cards at the top give you a quick snapshot of today's activity — how many patients are in the queue, open tasks, unread messages, and any case sheets waiting for attention."

> [Action: Slowly scroll down to the quick-action tiles]

"The quick-action strip here lets you jump directly to the most common tasks — starting a new intake, the appointment queue, calendar, messages, labwork, and your task list."

---

### [1:00–1:30] Sidebar navigation
> [Action: Point to / hover over the sidebar items one by one]

"On the left is the main navigation sidebar. The items you see depend on your role — not every role has access to every page. Admins will see additional options like User Management and Analytics that clinical staff won't."

> [Action: Click one sidebar item briefly, then return to dashboard]

"Throughout these training videos, you'll get a detailed walkthrough of each section. For now, just know that everything is one click away from the sidebar."

---

### [1:30–1:50] Display settings
> [Action: Click the gear icon in the top-right navbar]

"You can adjust your display settings from the gear icon at the top right — toggle between light and dark mode, change language, or increase the font size. These settings are available on every page."

> [Action: Toggle dark mode on briefly, then back off]

---

### [1:50–2:00] Closing
"That's the basics of logging in and getting oriented. Watch the role-specific videos in this series to learn the workflows that apply to your position."
